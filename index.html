About

Journalist who chose to go deeper

After 20 years building digital journalism at scale — editor-in-chief of TechTudo (Globo), coordinator of Globo.com, manager at NSC and Canaltech — I turned to academic research on sovereignty, disinformation and information warfare in post-Soviet Eastern Europe. Mentor in programmes by Google News Initiative and ICFJ/Meta. Speaker at SXSW (7×), Web Summit (6×), Slush Helsinki and global media events.
